# Suffolk Weekly Proof of Value Template

Use this template every week with the customer. Keep it short, factual, and tied to booked jobs.

The goal is not to celebrate activity. The goal is to show that Service Butler helped the team find real work, work the right leads, and attribute booked jobs back to the source.

Use the report as a customer-facing story:

- What we found
- Why it mattered
- What the team did
- What booked
- What we should do next
