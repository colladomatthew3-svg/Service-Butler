# ServiceButler Brand Guide

## Logo Usage
- `full` logo: use for nav bars, login, and marketing hero.
- `mark` logo: use for compact dashboard/mobile placements.
- Minimum height:
  - full logo: 28px
  - mark logo: 24px
- Keep clear space equal to at least half the logo mark height.

## Color Palette
- Brand Green: `#88BC42` (primary actions, focus identity)
- Brand Orange: `#DD9F74` (accent highlights, secondary emphasis)
- Neutrals:
  - Background: `#F6F8F7`
  - Surface: `#FFFFFF`
  - Surface Alt: `#F2F5F3`
  - Border: `#D8E0DB`
  - Text: `#1E2A24`
  - Muted: `#5B6C64`
- Semantic:
  - Success: `#1F9D55`
  - Warning: `#D99100`
  - Danger: `#D64545`

## Typography
- Headings: Manrope
- Body/UI: Inter
- Hierarchy:
  - H1: 44–56px, strong weight
  - H2: 30–36px, semibold
  - Body: 16px, readable line-height
  - Buttons: 14–16px semibold

## UI Components
- Buttons: 12px radius, green primary, neutral secondary, clear focus ring in green.
- Inputs/selects/textarea: 12px radius, subtle border, green focus glow.
- Cards: 14px radius, consistent header/body spacing, soft elevated shadow.

## Tone & Voice
- Friendly concierge for tradespeople.
- Action-oriented language: short, clear, practical.
- Avoid jargon-heavy “tech SaaS” phrasing.
