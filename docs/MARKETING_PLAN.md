# ServiceButler.ai Marketing Plan (MVP)

## 1) Positioning
**Core promise:** Help home-service businesses stop losing revenue from missed calls, slow follow-up, and dispatch gaps.

**Primary value proposition:**
- Capture leads from calls, SMS, and forms in one inbox.
- Automate immediate follow-up so hot leads do not go cold.
- Move leads to booked jobs faster with dispatch-friendly workflows.

**Suggested positioning line:**
> "From missed call to booked job in minutes."

## 2) ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Primary ICP
- US-based home-service companies with 3-50 field technicians.
- Verticals: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, and general service contractors.
- Operational pain: missed calls, delayed response times, and inconsistent dispatcher handoffs.

### Buyer + user roles
- **Buyer:** Owner/GM (cares about booked jobs, revenue, and team accountability).
- **Daily users:** Dispatcher/CSR and office manager.
- **Secondary users:** Technicians (job clarity and handoff quality).

## 3) Messaging Architecture
### Pillar A: Stop lead leakage
- "Never lose another lead."
- "Every inbound call/text gets captured and acted on."

### Pillar B: Speed to response
- "Auto-follow up in seconds, not hours."
- "Improve response-time SLA without adding headcount."

### Pillar C: Dispatch confidence
- "Clearer notes, better scheduling, fewer callbacks."
- "One dashboard for lead inbox, pipeline, and scheduling."

### Pillar D: ROI clarity
- "Recover missed-call revenue in the first month."
- "Track conversion lift from first response to booked job."

## 4) Offer Strategy
### Core offer
- 14-day free trial + guided setup.
- "Book your demo" CTA for higher-intent buyers.

### Risk-reversal
- "Setup in under one day" onboarding target.
- Optional "first 30 days optimization check-in" for early customers.

### Packaging (MVP-friendly)
- **Starter:** Lead capture + inbox + basic automation.
- **Growth:** Adds dispatch workflow + pipeline visibility.
- **Pro:** Adds advanced automations, reporting, and priority support.

## 5) Acquisition Channels (90-day rollout)
### Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Fast signal channels
1. **Founder-led outbound**
   - List-build 200 local/regional contractors by niche.
   - Outreach with pain-led message: missed calls → lost jobs.
2. **Local SEO landing pages**
   - Vertical pages (HVAC/plumbing/electrical) + city variants.
3. **Short-form proof content**
   - 30-60 second clips: "missed call to booked job" workflow.

### Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): Demand capture + retargeting
1. **Google Search Ads**
   - Keywords around missed calls, dispatch software, home service CRM alternatives.
2. **Meta retargeting**
   - Retarget site visitors with proof snippets and demo CTA.
3. **Case-study email sequences**
   - Nurture no-show demos and trial users.

### Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12): Partnerships + repeatable growth
1. **Industry partners**
   - Virtual assistants, call centers, marketing agencies serving contractors.
2. **Referral program**
   - Credit for customers/agencies introducing qualified accounts.
3. **Review flywheel**
   - Collect testimonials by vertical and company size.

## 6) Funnel Design
1. **TOFU:** Problem-aware ads/content ("Are missed calls costing you jobs?")
2. **MOFU:** Product tour + workflow proof + role-specific outcomes.
3. **BOFU:** Demo booking and trial start with onboarding checklist.
4. **Activation:** "First lead captured" and "first job scheduled" within 48 hours.

## 7) Website & Conversion Plan
### Homepage updates
- Keep headline anchored to lost-lead recovery.
- Add above-the-fold social proof and quantified outcomes.
- Add one dominant CTA per section (Demo or Start Trial).

### Conversion assets
- ROI calculator (estimate missed-call revenue recovered).
- One-page competitive comparison sheet.
- 3 vertical-specific case stories (HVAC, plumbing, electrical).

## 8) Content Plan (weekly cadence)
- **1x founder POV post:** operational bottlenecks in home services.
- **1x customer proof post:** before/after response time or booking rates.
- **1x tactical post:** scripts/templates for dispatch and follow-up.
- **1x product micro-demo:** specific workflow in under 90 seconds.

## 9) Sales Enablement
- Discovery script focused on call volume, missed-call %, and close rate.
- Demo script: show inbox → automation → schedule flow in <12 min.
- Objection handling library:
  - "We already use [tool]."
  - "Team won't adopt another system."
  - "Price concern."

## 10) KPIs & Targets (first 90 days)
### Pipeline metrics
- Website visitor → demo booking rate: **3-5%**
- Demo show rate: **65%+**
- Trial start rate from demos: **45%+**

### Activation metrics
- Time-to-first-lead-captured: **<48 hours**
- Time-to-first-job-scheduled: **<7 days**
- Week-2 active dispatcher rate: **70%+**

### Revenue metrics
- Trial-to-paid conversion: **20-30%**
- 90-day logo churn: **<10%**
- CAC payback target: **<3 months**
